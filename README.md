### Common AI Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Not editing AI output.** Always review and add your voice. Clients can smell unedited AI.
2. **Generic prompts â†’ generic outputs.** The more context you give, the better the output.
3. **One-shot prompting.** Iterate â€” "make it shorter," "more conversational," "add a specific example."
4. **Ignoring AI hallucinations.** Always fact-check claims, stats, and links.
5. **Not saving good prompts.** When a prompt works well, save the customized version.
6. **Using the wrong model.** Claude for long-form analysis, ChatGPT for quick tasks, Perplexity for research.
7. **Over-automating client-facing work.** AI drafts, you finalize. Clients pay for your judgment.
8. **Paying for tools you don't use.** Audit your subscriptions quarterly. Free tiers are often enough.
